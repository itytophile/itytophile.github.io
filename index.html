With Node.js

-- Aboprécis --

Aboprécis is a Node.js app whose goal is to filter videos of specific channels by keywords. This app is useful if your are interested in a single kind of videos from a youtuber who post a lot.
I primarily made this app to learn Javascript within Node.js. It also gave me the occasion to make a clean authentification system (I hope) with Passport and bcrypt (for password-hashing).
The CSS framework I used for the client side is Bulma.
